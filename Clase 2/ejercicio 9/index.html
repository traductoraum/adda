Sinopsis

A young girl, passionate about fashion design, is mysteriously able to enter the 1960s where she encounters her idol, a dazzling wannabe singer. But 1960s London is not what it seems, and time seems to fall apart with shady consequences. Our Take: Quiet since 2017's Baby Driver, director Edgar Wright (Shaun of the Dead, Scott Pilgrim vs. the World) is known for his hilariously quirky genre films. But his newest film, starring Anya Taylor-Joy and Matt Smith, is said to be a more straightforward horror-thriller inspired by British horror films like Don't Look Now. — Arno
